Problem statement:Organized artwork online

People do not have an easy "why" to organize pictures or screenshots of artworks online because there is no software that is catered to organizing artwork.

Affinity Diagram: ArtWork Organizer

My group and I worked together on this Jamboard to get ideas on what we are looking for in this application. We used ideas concerning Design, basic features of our app, organization, User Abilities, and the back end of our app. We wanted to create a streamlined thought process about what would be going into this application and have so far succeded in our goals.
